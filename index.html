Color and shape fitting AR-toy
The goal of this project is to provide an example of a three.js based application that uses an Augmented Reality (AR) library, AR.js. It was made to complete the grade of the MATA65 course, taught @UFBA.

Viewing the AR example will require a device with a camera and this image (either printed or displayed on a second device).
